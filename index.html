About Me

I am currently pursuing a graduate degree in Computer Science, focusing my research on quantum computing. A curiosity towards deep questions and a passion for learning and expanding knowledge drive me to leverage my skills and education to advance the field of Quantum Information Science. The transition from a decade in special operations to the tech sector has honed my skills in leadership and problem-solving. Collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, we strive to uncover data-driven opportunities that enhance operational efficiency. These experiences reflect my steadfast commitment to excellence and continuous learning in the dynamic field of data science. My current role as a Business Intelligence Analyst involves transforming complex datasets into strategic insights. I craft data visualizations that empower decision-making. My tenure as an Instructor at Mathnasium complements this role, where I refine my ability to simplify and communicate intricate concepts effectively.
